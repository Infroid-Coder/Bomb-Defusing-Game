Life lines

There are 3 lifelines you can use in this game.

Lifeline 1 adds 3 seconds to your timer.
Lifeline 2 adds 5 seconds to your timer.
Lifeline 3 adds 10 seconds to your timer.

Each of these lifelines reduce your score by a certain amount. The lifeline 1 reduces 100 from your score. Lifeline 2 reduces 200 from your score. Lifeline 3 reduces 300 from your score. Read More...
